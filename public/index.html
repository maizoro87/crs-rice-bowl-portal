About CRS Lenten Almsgiving

Our Lenten Journey

This Lent, our school community comes together to practice the three pillars of Lenten observance:

  • Prayer: Daily reflection on those who hunger
  • Fasting: Small sacrifices that add up
  • Almsgiving: Sharing our blessings with others

How to Participate

  1. Collect donations throughout Lent
  2. Donate online through the CRS link above
  3. Pray for those served by CRS programs worldwide
  4. Bring your class donations by the due date below

Collection Due Date

March 25, 2026

Please bring your donations to your theology class or the campus ministry office.
